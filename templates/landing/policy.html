1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth, BVN (for verification only), NIN (optional)
  • Verification Documents: Government ID (Driver's license, Voter's card, International passport), utility bills, proof of ownership
  • Profile Information: Bio, photo, employment details, references
  • Listing Information: Property details, photos, videos, documents, pricing
  • Communications: Messages with other users, support requests, survey responses
  • Payment Information: Bank account details (for payouts), transaction history

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information

  • • IP address & location data
  • • Browser type & version
  • • Operating system
  • • Device identifiers

Usage Information

  • • Pages viewed & time spent
  • • Search queries & filters
  • • Saved properties
  • • Click patterns

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Verification Services: Identity verification partners (YouVerify, IdentityPass)
  • Social Media: If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your public profile information
  • Fraud Databases: We may check against financial crime databases

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

1

To Provide Our Services

Create and manage your account, process property listings, facilitate communication between users, verify identities, process payments

2

To Improve Our Platform

Analyze usage patterns, develop new features, personalize your experience, conduct research and surveys

3

To Ensure Safety & Security

Detect and prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, investigate suspicious activities, maintain platform integrity

4

To Communicate With You

Service notifications, respond to inquiries, send updates about your listings or saved properties, marketing communications (with consent)

5

For Legal Compliance

Comply with applicable laws and regulations, respond to lawful requests from authorities, enforce legal claims

Legal Basis (NDPR): We process your personal information based on consent, contract performance, legal obligation, and legitimate interests. You may withdraw consent at any time.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

With Other Users

When you express interest in a property or initiate contact, your profile information (name, verification status) is shared with the other party. Property listings are public.

With Service Providers

We engage trusted third parties who process data on our behalf: cloud hosting (AWS/Azure), identity verification, payment processing, customer support tools, analytics (Google Analytics).

For Legal Reasons

When required by law, court order, or government request (NITDA, EFCC, Police). We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will require the acquiring entity to honor this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access controls. Employees access user data only when necessary for support or development.

Audit Logs

All access to personal data is logged and regularly audited for unauthorized activity.

Compliance

We comply with ISO 27001 standards and undergo annual third-party security audits.

No method of transmission is 100% secure. While we implement industry-leading security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Opt-out of marketing communications and withdraw consent for non-essential processing.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Submit a request through any of these channels:

  • privacy@myhousepadi.com
  • myhousepadi.com/privacy/requests
  • 24 Bishop Aboyade Cole, Victoria Island, Lagos (Attn: Data Protection Officer)

We will respond within 7 working days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with NITDA (www.nitda.gov.ng).

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

Active Accounts

Data retained until account deletion or 24 months of inactivity.

Deleted Accounts

Anonymized data retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted. Legal/financial records retained for 6 years (CAMA requirement).

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for analytics and product improvement.

8. Children's Privacy

House Padi is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@myhousepadi.com.

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in Nigeria (AWS Africa - Cape Town) and the European Union. For users in Nigeria, your data is primarily processed within Nigeria. When we transfer data outside Nigeria, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Binding Corporate Rules (for affiliates)
  • Adequacy decisions by NITDA

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email and platform notification at least 14 days before becoming effective.
