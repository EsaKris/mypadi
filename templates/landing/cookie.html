Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser's help section:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies

Opt-Out Tools

Industry opt-out programs:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)
  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Important: Blocking essential cookies may prevent some platform features from working properly, such as login and saved properties.

Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers transmit "Do Not Track" signals. Currently, no uniform standard for responding to DNT signals exists. We respond to DNT signals by continuing our current practices unless a legal standard is established.
